Jori's Purpose

Jori is a gamified personal management app designed for everyone but with a special focus on those with student and academic lives. During this point of our lives it can be overwhelming to care for anything beyond academia responsibilities, but at the end of the day we are people with lives beyond our education career. Jori organizes your tasks so you can see the true capabilities of your time and helps you find the perfect jori between the aspects of your life that make you, you.

Our Features

how it works

The process of time management and productivity built into Jori follows the Eisenhower Matrix, a task management tool, that has you sort tasks based on urgency and priority. The methodology of Jori is to first dump out all your tasks to prevent mental blocks, schedule your tasks to give you certainty and realistic guidelines, and then help you follow through with your plans with a simple and sleek workspace interface. Generally, Jori is like a second brain, it takes away the effort one normally puts in to remember deadlines, to-dos, healthy habits, etc.
